Welcome to the Galaxy!

Your goal is to bring happiness to the galaxy - through TRADE.

Providing planets with enough basic resources and an abundance of their favorite resource will make that planet happy. When all planets are happy, you win.

Each planet produces one unique resource and has one favorite resource. These resources are more expensive, but provide much more happiness, so be strategic in your purchasing.
